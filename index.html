About Me

As a dedicated **DevSecOps & CyberArk Architect** with over a decade of experience, I specialize in forging robust, secure, and highly automated infrastructure. My expertise spans the entire development lifecycle, focusing on integrating stringent security measures into CI/CD pipelines from inception. I leverage profound knowledge in **CyberArk Privileged Access Management (PAM)** solutions, **Kubernetes GitOps workflows**, and **Infrastructure as Code (IaC) with Terraform** to empower organizations to accelerate innovation while maintaining uncompromised security posture.

I am committed to designing and implementing scalable cloud security architectures on **AWS**, ensuring compliance and operational excellence. My approach emphasizes proactive threat mitigation, automated vulnerability management, and fostering a collaborative DevSecOps culture that shifts security "left" without impeding development velocity.
